Paragon Internship Guidelines

Key Points to Remember

1
Working Hours
  • HO & DC: Mon-Thu 07:30-17:00 | Fri 07:30-16:15
  • Plant: Mon-Thu 07:00-16:00 | Fri 07:00-16:30
  • Break time: 12:00-13:00 + 15 min Ashar prayer (Fri 11:30-13:00)
  • Weekly target: 40 working hours per week
  • Flexible hour arrangement:
    • Missing hours due to classes/leave? → Compensate after office hours or another day
    • Working overtime one day? → Claim time off the next day or another day
    • Key principle: Mix and match your hours flexibly, as long as you hit 40 hours weekly
  • Event allowance (holidays/red days only): If you've completed 40 hours in a week and work on holidays/red days for company events (activations, offline events with KOLs, corporate events, etc.), you may claim event allowance for minimum 4 hours. Note: Regular overtime for desk work is NOT claimable—handle that during office hours
2
Allowance Payment Cycle
  • Attendance cycle: 21st (start) → 20th (end)
  • Track your own attendance daily
  • On the 21st: HR sends template, copy-paste from your personal records and submit to the link shared monthly
  • Allowance is paid on the last day of the month
  • If you join outside the cycle (not on the 21st), your allowance will be prorated accordingly
3
NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)
⚠️ Mandatory Compliance
  • Protect all confidential company information (data, documents, systems)
  • DO NOT disclose information to third parties under any circumstances
  • Remains effective after internship ends
  • All work outputs become Company property
  • Maintain ParagonCorp's reputation and good name
  • Please ensure only authorized and legitimate applications are used when connected to Paragon's network
  • Violations may result in legal action
4
Final Presentation
  • Must be conducted max. 15 days (D-15) before internship period ends
  • Participants arrange their own schedule with their Supervisor
  • Main evaluator: Supervisor (supported by HR/L&D if needed)
  • Submit PPT deck via bit.ly/ParagonInternFinal at the latest 1 day before (D-1)
  • Deck submission = requirement for internship certificate
  • Assessment criteria: project understanding, analysis, execution & impact, communication, growth mindset
